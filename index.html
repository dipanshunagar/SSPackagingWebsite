About Us

We specialize in packaging material. i.e. PE/HM Plain Poly Bags, Printed Poly Bags, Rolls, Poly Sheets, PE Cover 3D, PE Cover Elastic, PE Adhesive Film, Stretch Film, POF Shrink Film, Air Bubble Bag/Roll, EPP Foam, EVA Foam etc. which are widely used in the industrial sector and serving them since 2010.
